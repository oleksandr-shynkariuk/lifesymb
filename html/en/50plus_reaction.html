ReAction


Focus: Speed and lightness
Methods: ReAction training; Wing Tsun training; Complex movements; Active fall prevention

Short description: : The ReAction course is an important step on the way to physical mastery. With the aid of our ReAction trainer it is possible to simulate real life situations such as falling forward, falling backwards, jumping and flying. The participants will also learn more advanced Wing Tsun techniques and train them even blindfold. Everything is guided by our certified instructors, which turns the course into a unique experience.
When muscle recruitment is less than optimal, that can be a sign of anything from injury to compensation to poor motor learning. If a person doesn’t use the movements that are available, then they will be forgotten. Especially in higher ages, when the ability to react fast is decreasing, it is important to put the body in uncertain situations. By doing so the body has to react, rather than anticipate (this is the ability of the body to predict a future situation), and increases the array of possible solutions to challenging tasks in the daily life.

ReAction traning
Our reaction trainer consists of elastic straps, which are spread over two sides. Depending on the participant’s body weight, one can also add or remove up to two bands on each side.
To reduce the impact of the bodyweight on different movements, we choose exercises where the bands are working towards the gravity. This allows the participant to experience an ease in the body, and learn and train movements, speed and agility that would not have been possible otherwise.
