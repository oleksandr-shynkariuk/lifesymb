When you join the LifeSymb program you have all possibilities to choose the right program that suits you best. You can choose the advanced program, where you have one therapy treatment included or the basic program. The basic program includes only training, and you have to pay additionally for additional services. However, if you choose the premium program you have 2 treatments and the LifeSymb Analysis program included and all additional services are reduced by 10%. See the price section for more details.

Who needs LifeSymb Analysis?

LifeSymb Analysis is a program for persons with acute or chronic impairments. All treatments and services are mild, gentle and especially optimized for the demands of the young at heart (50+). You can always choose to take the LifeSymb courses only. However, if you have one or more of the following complaints, you should consider the LifeSymb Analysis program.

  • Knee problems
  • Back pain
  • Hip impairments
  • Osteoporosis
  • Shoulder impairments (e.g. pain while lifting)
  • Muscular imbalances (e.g. tennis elbow)
  • Physical abrasion (e.g. slipped disc)
  • Instability (e.g. lack of balance)

In many cases more than one of these impairments is present at the same time. Wrong movement patterns and imbalances are often the root of the problem. That is where LifeSymb Analysis comes into play.

How does LifeSymb Analysis work?

You might have exercised with other practitioners before or maybe this is your first step in taking initiative for the sake of your well-being. In all cases, welcome to LifeSymb and this is how it works.

When you decide to take part in the LifeSymb Analysis program, the first step is to define your current physical situation. During a free meeting in our office we are setting your goals and the methods to achieve it.

We use the following methods:
  • Functional movement screening (FMS)
  • Movement analysis
  • Endurance tests
  • Strength tests
  • Feldenkrais therapy
  • Physiotherapy

Mostly not all of the tests will be necessary and we are trying to keep the effort for you as low as possible. As soon as this is done, we guide you to the training.

What makes LifeSymb Analysis special?

After we set your goals and measured your health situation it is time to begin with the training and/or treatment. We don't want to send you home together with a mass of exercises and leave you all alone with that. Neither do we want you to spend all of your savings on a personal exercise program. So we came up with the idea to design personal training in groups. In that way we create a convenient atmosphere with the possibility to get in contact with people of your kind, while you work on your individual goals. Both Symbalance and Gravity are unique forms of training. These courses are based on compelling technological and traditional solutions that have successfully been used by people all over the world. To increase your peace of mind we also offer meditation, yoga, movement therapy, SEP therapy, nutrition consulting, and others.

How do I get started?

The LifeSymb Analysis program is only available within the framework of the premium program. This makes sense since the training takes at least 6 weeks to show clear results. Contact us now and experience the LifeSymb program.

What if I don't like the LifeSymb Services?

Our courses are specifically designed for the individual demands of your customers. Further this training will be offered either in groups or as personal training. We try to improve the training together with our customers and offer discounts for the participation in the development of new methods. Before you sign in to the LifeSymb program, you are welcome to attempt to a trial training. In that way you can make sure to test us before you pay.
