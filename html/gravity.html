Gravity

The "Gravity" program is based on a new German technology called “4D Pro”. It is created by Dr. med Dr. disc.pol. Homayun Gharavi, and it literally changes the force of gravity and so the load of our own body. These changes can either reduce or increase the intensity of the training.

In that way, the gravity program represents an excellent alternative to conventional training, especially for people with physical constraints or higher ages.

Training in 3 different intensities:
  • Low (rehab)
  • Middle (maintenance)
  • High (performance)
