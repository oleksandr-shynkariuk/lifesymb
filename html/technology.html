Element

NoGravity - Coordination+ - Rehab

  • NoGravity

    The "NoGravity" program literally changes the force of gravity and so the load of the own body. These changes can either reduce or increase the intensity of the training. Ii is so an excellent alternative to conventional training, especially for people with physical constraints or higher ages.

  • Coordination+

    The 4D allows the trainees to do movements with different resistence ond each side. If the band is stronger on one side, than the other side has to compensate this situation with coordinative task that forces the body to improve its coordination. Muscular imbalances, coordinative weaknesses and loss of balance is now a thing of the past.

  • Rehab

    The LifeSymb rehab program is different from other rehab programs. Instead of long breaks without being able to move in a normal way, we bring the patient back in reality faster and even stronger than before. We use the 4D technology to reduce weight on the movements, which allows the patient to start training the muscle and other supportive tissue in the most functional way.
    It is not going to be a boring rehab training. It is going to be a LIFESYMB REHAB!
